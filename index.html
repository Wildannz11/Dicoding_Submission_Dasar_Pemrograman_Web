Players

These are examples of well-known sports players in their respective fields

Football Player

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro was born on 5 February 1985. He is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for English Premier League club Manchester United and captains the Portugal national team.

Volleyball Player

Earvin N'Gapeth (born 12 February 1991) is a French volleyball player, member of the France national volleyball team and Iranian club Paykan Tehran VC, 2015 European Champion, gold medallist of the World League (2015 and 2017), French Champion (2010), Italian Champion (2016) and Olympic Champion (2020).

Tennis Player

Rafael "Rafa" Nadal Parera (born 3 June 1986) is a professional tennis player from Manacor, Mallorca, Spain. After being ranked number two in the world for 158 weeks, on August 18, 2008 he became the world's first ranked player to replace Roger Federer. The ranking lasted until July 6, 2009 when Federer won Wimbledon and was again world number one.

Basketball Player

Michael Jeffrey Jordan (born February 17, 1963) is an American professional basketball player. He is a world famous player in the sport. At least, six times won the NBA championship with the Chicago Bulls (1991-1993, 1996-1998). He has a height of 198 cm and won the best player title.
